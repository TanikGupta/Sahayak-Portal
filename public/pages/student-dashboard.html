📋 University Entry–Exit Procedures

Students are not permitted to leave the campus between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM.
Between 6:00 AM and 10:00 PM, students may move in and out of the campus freely.
For outstation travel, students must apply for leave via email to the Hostel Warden or submit a leave form at least four hours prior to the scheduled departure to avoid inconvenience.

📋 Night Attendance

Night attendance must be marked between 10:00 PM and 10:30 PM.
In case of a student fails to mark attendance, the hostel authorities will inform the concerned parents/guardian.
Repeated failure to mark attendance will attract disciplinary action, including fines.

📖 Registration Guidelines for Newly Admitted Students

JK Lakshmipat University, Jaipur — Academic Affairs

📄 Document Preparation

Receive the original mark sheet and passing certificate of XII/Qualifying Examination from the respective Board/University.
Check your Aadhar details with your marksheet and offer letter. In case of any change, apply to update Aadhar Card immediately. Visit UIDAI
Check the phone number linked to your Aadhar Card and update if needed.

🔗 Digital Accounts to Set Up

Open an account on Digilocker if not already. Retrieve your X and XII Marksheet from your respective board.
Create your APAAR ID on abc.gov.in. Select admission year "2025" and Institute as "JK Lakshmipat University, Jaipur". Apply as "New Admission".

🚫 Anti-Ragging

JKLU is a ragging-free campus. Fill the anti-ragging undertaking at antiragging.in
Print the filled form and submit at the time of registration, signed by you and your parents in original. No scanned signatures permitted.
