University Entry–Exit Procedures Date:                      
  • Students are not permitted to leave the campus between 10:00 PM and 6:00 AM.
  • Between 6:00 AM and 10:00 PM, students may move in and out of the campus freely.
  • For outstation travel, students must apply for leave via email to the Hostel Warden or submit a leave form at least four hours prior to the scheduled departure to avoid inconvenience.
Night Attendance
  • Night attendance must be marked between 10:00 PM and 10:30 PM.
  • In case a student fails to mark attendance, the hostel authorities will inform the concerned parents/guardian.
  • Repeated failure to mark attendance will attract disciplinary action, including fines.
