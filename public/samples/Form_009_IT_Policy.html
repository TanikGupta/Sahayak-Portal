GENERAL EXPECTATIONS
Candidates should have a good general physique. In particular,
□  Chest measurement should not be less than 70 cm, with satisfactory limits of expansion and contraction.
□  Vision should be normal. In case of defective vision, it should be corrected to 6/9 in both eyes or 6/6 in the better eye. Colour blind and uniocular (having vision in only one eye) persons are restricted from admission to certain courses.
□  Hearing should be normal. Defective hearing should be corrected.
□  Heart and lungs should not have any abnormality and there should be no history of mental illness and epileptic fits.
